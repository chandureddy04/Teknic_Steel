Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 17, 2025

Who We Are

Teknic Steel ("we", "us", "our") is a structural steel detailing company providing 3D modeling, shop drawing production, and BIM coordination for fabricators and contractors. We operate as a small, senior team based in India and serve US and global fabricators.

Information We Collect

  • Contact details you submit (name, company, email, phone).
  • Project information (drawings, models, RFIs, specifications, schedules).
  • Files you upload through our forms (PDF, DWG/DXF, IFC, RVT, images).
  • Basic usage data (device, browser, pages visited) for site reliability and security.

How We Use Your Information

  • Respond to inquiries and provide quotes after reviewing your drawings.
  • Deliver detailing services, coordination, and agreed project deliverables.
  • Operate, secure, and improve our website and services.
  • Comply with applicable laws, regulations, and contractual obligations.

Legal Basis (where applicable)

  • Performance of a contract or pre‑contractual steps (quotes, service delivery).
  • Legitimate interests (service quality, security, fraud prevention).
  • Consent (where required, e.g., certain cookies or marketing).

Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with trusted service providers that help us operate our business (e.g., hosting, email, file storage). These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only as instructed. We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights and the rights of others.

International Transfers

We are based in India and may process data in India and other locations where our service providers operate. If you are located outside these regions, your information may be transferred internationally. We apply reasonable safeguards to protect your information during such transfers.

Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as needed for the purposes described above, to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Project records may be kept for a reasonable period for reference and quality purposes.

Security

We employ reasonable technical and organizational measures designed to protect the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of your information. No method of transmission or storage is fully secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Cookies and Similar Technologies

Our website may use minimal cookies or similar technologies to maintain basic functionality and improve reliability. You can control cookies through your browser settings. We do not use cookies to sell personal information.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict the processing of your personal information. To exercise these rights, contact us at sales@teknicsteel.com. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

Children’s Privacy

Our services are intended for business users and are not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will revise the “Last updated” date above and encourage you to review this page periodically.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, contact us at sales@teknicsteel.com.
