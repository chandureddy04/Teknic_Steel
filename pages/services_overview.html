Our SDS/2 expertise reduces project delivery time by 40% while maintaining industry-leading accuracy standards. Automated clash detection and material optimization ensure efficient fabrication.

Connection Types

Moment Connections

Rigid connections transferring both shear and moment forces. Ideal for high-rise buildings and structures requiring lateral stability.

Shear Connections

Simple connections transferring vertical loads only. Cost-effective solution for standard beam-to-column connections.

Braced Frame Connections

Specialized connections for lateral force-resisting systems. Essential for seismic and wind load resistance.
